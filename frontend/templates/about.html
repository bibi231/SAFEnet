About SAFEnet

Secure Anonymous Feedback & Emergency Network

SAFEnet is a secure web-based platform designed to enable anonymous emergency and incident reporting in Nigeria. The platform aims to protect user identity through encryption and anonymization while connecting victims to verified support services.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between victims of gender-based violence and the support they need by providing a safe, secure, and anonymous reporting channel.

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. SAFEnet is built in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023. We employ strict data minimization practices and end-to-end encryption to ensure your report remains confidential.
