About SAFEnet

Secure Anonymous Feedback & Emergency Network

Our Mission

SAFEnet was created to provide a safe, secure, and anonymous platform for reporting incidents and emergencies in Abuja, Nigeria. We believe that everyone deserves access to help and support, regardless of their circumstances or ability to identify themselves.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our top priority. SAFEnet is designed with privacy-by-design principles:

  • We do not collect IP addresses or personal identifiers
  • Reports are submitted completely anonymously
  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We comply with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023
  • Our platform is regularly audited for security

Our Partners

We work with verified NGOs, hospitals, legal aid organizations, and emergency services throughout Abuja to ensure you get the help you need. All partner organizations undergo thorough verification before being listed on our platform.

For Service Providers

Are you an organization that provides emergency services, legal aid, medical assistance, or other support services? Register your organization to join our network of verified providers.

Contact Us

For questions, feedback, or support:
Email: support@safenet.ng
Emergency: Call 112 (Police, Fire, Ambulance)

Technology

SAFEnet is built with modern web technologies including Flask, PostgreSQL, and implements industry-standard security practices. Our codebase is regularly updated and maintained to ensure the highest levels of security and reliability.
